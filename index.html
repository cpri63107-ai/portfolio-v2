Hi, I'm
Devipriya.

For nearly three years, I had the chance to bring some warmth, wit and humour into the B2B marketing communications space, working at a creative agency that primarily serviced software giants.

I added to brands, expanding, growing, and acting as custodian of the clients we served. The breadth of what I worked on ranged from public-byline thought leadership and ghostwritten executive blogs, to sponsorship activations across Formula E and the Australian Open, to interactive ebooks, branded films, and graphic novels.

I'm now finishing an MSc in Marketing Science at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, sharpening the data half of the craft. I'm looking out for roles in marketing communications and brand communications. Roles that put both halves to work.
